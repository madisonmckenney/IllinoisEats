Unearthing Food Insecurity Resources

The Greater Chicago Food Depository

Chicago and its surrounding neighbors face the issue of hunger in high numbers. In order to combat this, the Greater Chicago Food Depository has made it their mission to end hunger in their communities by working with a netwok=rk of community based organizations and individuals.

Feeding Illinois

Through a vast number of networks and resources, members of Feeding Illinois provide food and other services to every county in Illinois. To see how they manage to make it work, visit their page linked above!

Illinois Commission to End Hunger

Established in 2010, the Illinois Commission to End Hungeris a public-private partnership composed of stakeholders from across the state dedicated to the belief that no one in Illinois should ever face hunger. To read more about their mission, visit their page linked above!

Bigger Table

Launched in 2020, Bigger Table, a non-profit organization located in the Chicagoland area, aims to bring together the food and beverage industry to address hunger, unemployment, and inclusive economic development. To read more about their mission, visit their page as linked above!
